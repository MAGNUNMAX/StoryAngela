It was the time of UFOs, it was the time of little green men.
In a small town nestled between rolling hills, people often spoke of strange lights in the sky. It was the time of UFOs, and whispers of little green men filled the air. One night, young Emma sat by her window, gazing at the stars, when a soft hum echoed through the quiet streets.

A shimmering disc hovered above her backyard, its glow casting an eerie light. From it emerged tiny figures—green, just like in the stories. But instead of terror, they waved at her with small hands and curious eyes. Emma waved back.

They didn’t come to invade, just to say hello.
